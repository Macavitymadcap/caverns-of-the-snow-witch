The Snow Witch laughs, saying, 'You are an amusing little fellow, but I have a game for you to play that you will not win.' She concentrates her power on Redswift and Stubb and their metal collars tighten. They both clutch their throats as if gasping for breath. You struggle to your feet, yelling insults at the Snow Witch, mocking her cowardly way of dealing with defenceless slaves. You challenge her to a combat, of whatever type she wishes. She releases her stranglehold on Redswift and Stubb, saying, 'Even though I have beaten you, I enjoy games. I will play!' Suddenly you hear the sound of shuffling footsteps coming from the tunnel opposite. An Elf and a Dwarf enter the cavern: they look almost identical to Redswift and Stubb except that their vacant looks and putrid white flesh indicate that they are both ZOMBIES. The Snow Witch tells you to fight them while she invents a game for you to play. The Zombies lumber forward and you are forced to fight the terrible replicas of your friends.

  • DWARF ZOMBIE SKILL 8 STAMINA 9
  • ELF ZOMBIE SKILL 9 STAMINA 9

Fight both the Zombies at the same time. Each will have a separate attack on you during each Attack Round, but you must choose which of the two you will fight. Attack your nominated target as in a normal battle. Against the other, you will throw for your Attack Strength in the normal way, but you will not wound it if your Attack Strength is greater; you must count this as though you have just parried its blow. Of course, if its Attack Strength is greater, it will have wounded you in the normal way. If you win, turn to 23.
