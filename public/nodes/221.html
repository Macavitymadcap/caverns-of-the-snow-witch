The Mountain Elf sighs with disappointment and walks across to the wall to open a hidden door. 'You should have been more trusting,' he says, 'I am in debt to the Healer for curing my frostbite, and was just about to send a messenger with my payment. But don't worry, the Healer lives at the summit of the Moonstone Hills. Follow this tunnel - it will lead you outside to the river. Follow the river bank to the east and you will reach the hills. You can't miss them. Goodbye and good LUCK!'. You thank the Elf for his kindness and set off down the tunnel (turn to 38).
