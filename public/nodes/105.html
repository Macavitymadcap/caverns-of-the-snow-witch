Ash waits for an opportunity to shoot and fires another arrow at close range. This time he does not miss, and the Death Hawk plunges to the ground with an arrow protruding from its breast. The other side of the hill slopes down into a gorge and Ash tells you that you must now journey alone to meet the Healer. He tells you to walk east up the gorge until you see the head of a Phoenix carved in the rock. The Healer lives in a cave in the side of the hill above the carved rock. You thank Ash for his help and walk slowly down the hill into the gorge. At the bottom you follow Ash's instructions and turn left up the gorge (turn to 252).
