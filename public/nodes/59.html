Before you stands a CRYSTAL WARRIOR, one of the Snow Witch's personal guardians who has been sent to deal with you. He is made of quartz which has been animated by the Snow Witch's sorcery. Edged weapons will not harm the Crystal Warrior - your sword is useless! If you possess a war-hammer you may succeed in smashing the Crystal Warrior to pieces.

  • CRYSTAL WARRIOR SKILL 11 STAMINA 13

If you do not possess a war-hammer, turn to 2. If you win, turn to 148.
