As you pull up the vine into the light, you see that it is crawling with revolting FLESH GRUBS, their blind heads writhing around in search of living flesh in which to burrow. You release the vine, but one of the Grubs crawls on to the back of your hand, Fortunately you manage to pull it off and stamp it underfoot. Add 1 LUCK point. You will not find the Healer in the tree stump so you decide to carry on walking up the river valley (turn to 119).
