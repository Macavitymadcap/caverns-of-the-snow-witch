You open your backpack and reluctantly pay the man 50 Gold Pieces. Still smiling, he takes a jar from a shelf and pours the contents into a pan of soup which is simmering on an open fire in his kitchen. He fills a bowl with his herbal remedy and hands it to you, saying, 'That should do the job. It will take about an hour to work, so I suggest you go and lie down in the sun by the river. You'll soon be feeling much better.' You gulp down the soup, thank him curtly, and walk back down the footpath to find a spot to rest (turn to 209).
