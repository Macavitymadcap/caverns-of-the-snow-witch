You hurriedly cut blocks of ice out of the mountain-side and build a makeshift igloo. You crawl into it as the blizzard blows down the mountain with ferocious power. Your body-heat is retained inside the igloo and you keep warm. However, you must eat two portions of your Provisions to regain your strength after the tiring walk and the effort of building the igloo (this does not increase your STAMINA). An hour later, the blizzard dies down and you crawl out of your shelter to continue your quest (turn to 169).
