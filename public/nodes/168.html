The footpath twists and turns through the trees before ending outside a wooden hut. You creep up to the hut and look through the window. An old man wearing purple robes and a grey skull cap is asleep in an ornately carved rocking-chair. The shelves on the wall behind him are lined with jars of herbs and berries. If you wish to enter the hut, turn to 341. If you would rather walk back down the path to continue up the valley, turn to 205.
