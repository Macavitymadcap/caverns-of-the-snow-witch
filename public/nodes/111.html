In the distance you can hear chanting voices; soon the tunnel ends at the entrance to a large cavern. Kneeling down before an ice effigy in the shape of a demon, with their hooded faces pressed to the ice floor in worship, are ten of the Snow Witch's followers. There are two exits from the cave, one straight ahead and one to your right. If you wish to pretend to be one of the worshippers and walk boldly into the cavern, turn to 300. If you would rather creep cautiously into the cavern, turn to 283.
