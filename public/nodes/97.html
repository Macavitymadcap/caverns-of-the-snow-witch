There is no writing in the book, just a recess cut in the pages which holds a talisman on a golden chain. The talisman is a jade frog. If you wish to put the chain around your neck, turn to 327. If you would prefer to leave it in the book, you may, if you have not done so already, do one of the following:

  • Blow the flute. Turn to 74
  • Read the runes on the stick. Turn to 345
  • Smell the rose. Turn to 317

    • If you do not wish to do any of the above, you may leave the cave and turn left into the tunnel (turn to 198).
