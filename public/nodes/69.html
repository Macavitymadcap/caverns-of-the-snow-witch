69

After putting the coins carefully into a hidden pocket, the old man tells you to be careful of two things if you are travelling south to Stonebridge. Firstly, the nearest water-hole has been poisoned; and secondly, there are lots of Hill Trolls gathering to the north of Stonebridge. He bids you good luck and farewell and walks off. Stubb urges you to set off as quickly as possible, worried about the possible attack on Stonebridge by the Hill Trolls (turn to 348).
