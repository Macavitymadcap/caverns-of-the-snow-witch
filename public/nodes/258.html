The energy bolt which hits the globe sends out a brilliant flash of light that makes you turn your head away. You open your eyes to find that the Snow Witch has vanished! You are momentarily stunned by the Snow Witch's disappearance. Suddenly you hear the sound of shuffling footsteps coming from the tunnel opposite. An Elf and a Dwarf enter the cavern: they look almost identical to Redswift and Stubb except that their vacant looks and putrid white flesh indicate that they are both ZOMBIES. The Snow Witch has unleashed her magical powers to make you fight the terrible replicas of your friends.

  • DWARF ZOMBIE SKILL 8 STAMINA 9
  • ELF ZOMBIE SKILL 9 STAMINA 9

Fight both the Zombies at the same time. During each Attack Round, they will both make a separate attack on you, but you must choose which of the two you will fight. Attack your nominated target as in a normal battle. Against the other, you will throw for your Attack Strength in the normal way, but you will not wound it if your Attack Strength is greater; you must count this as though you have just parried its blow. Of course, if its Attack Strength is greater, it will have wounded you in the normal way. If you win, turn to 23.
