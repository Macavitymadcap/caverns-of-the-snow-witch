You are almost at the entrance to the tunnel when the worshippers stop their chanting. They stand up and one of them calls out to you asking why you did not stop to sing the praises of The Frozen One. If you have a magic flute, you can tell them that you have been ordered to go and play it for the Snow Witch (turn to 31). Otherwise you may either fight them (turn to 143), or try to run for the tunnel (turn to 33).
