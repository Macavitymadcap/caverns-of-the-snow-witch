One of the Hill Trolls looks up and sees you walking by. He gives a cry of alarm and the three of them run out of the trees to attack. You have to fight two of them.

  • First HILL TROLL SKILL 8 STAMINA 9
  • Second HILL TROLL SKILL 9 STAMINA 9

Fight them both at the same time. During each Attack Round, they will both make a separate attack on you, but you must choose which one you will fight. Attack your chosen Hill Troll as in a normal battle. Against the other one you will throw for your Attack Strength in the normal way, but you will not wound it if your Attack Strength is greater; you must just count this as though you have defended yourself against its blow. Of course, if its Attack Strength is greater, it will wound you. If you defeat them both, turn to 164.
