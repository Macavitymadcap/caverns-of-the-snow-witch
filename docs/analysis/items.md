- **Garlic** (Nodes 210, 297 - protection against Vampire)

- **Magic flute** (Nodes 299, 31, 370 - can trick guards/followers)

- **Copper ring** (Node 313 - summons warrior to fight White Dragon)
- **Elfin boots** (Nodes 128, 374 - silent movement)
- **Jade frog talisman/Amulet of Courage** (Nodes 189, 365 - +2 SKILL, protects against Brain Slayer)

## Notes on Item Management

1. **Backpack capacity is limited** - At node 171, for every 50 Gold Pieces taken, one item must be left behind
2. **Some items are exclusive choices** - You can only pick one ring initially, or choose between sword and spear
3. **Items can be lost** - Shields can be lost when falling in water, items broken during Air Elemental attack
4. **Some items are one-time use** - Copper ring (summon warrior), scrolls, potions
5. **Equipment can be damaged** - Sword can shatter (node 261), items can be lost to acid or water

## Critical Item Paths

### To Defeat Snow Witch (Vampire Form):
1. **Garlic** (from node 200) - weakens her
2. **Carved rune stick** (from node 194) - stake to kill her
3. Must have SKILL >10 to successfully stake her (nodes 7, 34)

### To Win Final Game:
- Need all three **metal discs** (square, circular, star)
- Found at nodes 101 (square), 207 (circular), 141 (star)

### To Break Death Spell:
1. **Dragon's egg** (optional, from node 200) - for Healer's concoction
2. **Candle and tinder-box** (from node 342) - to cross pit safely
3. **Silver object** (any) - to summon Pegasus to Firetop Mountain
4. **Mask of Life** (provided by Healer at node 75)
