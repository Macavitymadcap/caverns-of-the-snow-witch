# Battle Sequences in "Caverns of the Snow Witch"

## Node 13 - Hill Trolls

**Combatants**:

- First HILL TROLL: SKILL 9, STAMINA 10
- Second HILL TROLL: SKILL 9, STAMINA 9

**Special Conditions**:

Fight both at the same time. During each Attack Round, both make separate
attacks but you must choose which to fight. Attack your chosen troll normally;
against the other, roll for Attack Strength but only defend (don't wound it if
your strength is greater). If its Attack Strength is greater, it wounds you
normally.

## Node 17 - Mountain Elf

**Combatant**:

- MOUNTAIN ELF: SKILL 6, STAMINA 6

**Special Conditions**:

If the Mountain Elf's STAMINA falls to 2, turn to 305.

## Node 20 - Cave-Man

**Combatant**:

- CAVE-MAN: SKILL 8, STAMINA 8

**Special Conditions**:

May Escape after two Attack Rounds by running to catch up with Redswift and
Stubb.

## Node 37 - Night Stalker

**Combatant**:

- NIGHT STALKER: SKILL 11, STAMINA 8

**Special Conditions**:

During each round of combat, reduce your Attack Strength by 2 because you are
not used to fighting in the dark.

## Node 108 - Ice Demon

**Combatant**:

- ICE DEMON: SKILL 9, STAMINA 11

**Special Conditions**:

In addition to normal attack, throw one die every Attack Round for freezing gas
from its nostrils. On 1, 2, or 3, the gas hits and reduces STAMINA by 1 point.
On 4, 5, or 6, the gas misses.

## Node 185 - Banshee

**Combatant**:

- BANSHEE: SKILL 12, STAMINA 12

**Special Conditions**:

Before each Attack Round, roll two dice. If the total is same or less than your
SKILL, you can fight normally. If total is greater than your SKILL, you are
paralyzed by fear and automatically lose that Attack Round.

**Combatant**:
